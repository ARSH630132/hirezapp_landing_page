# GFF AI project guardrails

- Do not modify the approved landing page unless the current task explicitly says landing changes are allowed.
- Never reintroduce app/[...slug]/page.tsx.
- Never recreate components/PublicRoutePage.tsx.
- Public routes must remain explicit App Router pages.
- Do not add backend, FastAPI, AWS, auth, real OTP, real email, database, real exports, or credentials.
- Do not invent fake clients, fake logos, fake metrics, fake reports, fake downloads, fake authors, fake office addresses, or fake dashboards.
- Use frontend-only placeholder states where backend behavior is needed later.
- Preserve the approved landing page hero and right-side video.
- Use premium, enterprise, Apple-like UI.
- Avoid box-inside-box clutter, excessive pills, badge soup, neon overload, and generic robot imagery.
- Ensure mobile responsiveness and accessibility.
